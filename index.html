Privacy Policy - Serenity Sounds

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Serenity Sounds mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. The App is a meditation and ambient-sound app published by Peter Hussaini.

1. Information we collect

The App is designed to collect as little as possible. Depending on how you use it:

  • Account information (only if you choose to sign in with Google): your name, email address, and profile picture, as provided by Google. You can use the App fully as a guest without signing in.
  • Purchase information: when you buy a subscription or a one-time purchase, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a record of which entitlement you own (via our billing provider, RevenueCat) but never your full card or payment details - those are handled entirely by Google.
  • On-device data: your preferences, onboarding answers, and session history are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and personalise the App experience.
  • To deliver premium features to subscribers and restore purchases across devices.
  • To keep the App working reliably and to fix problems.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Third-party services

The App relies on the following services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Billing & Google Sign-In - policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) - revenuecat.com/privacy

4. Data retention and deletion

Locally stored data is removed when you uninstall the App or sign out. To request deletion of any account data associated with your Google sign-in, contact us at the email below and we will delete it within 30 days.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You can also revoke Google access at any time in your Google Account settings.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact
