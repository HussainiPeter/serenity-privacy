Delete your data - Serenity Sounds

App: Serenity Sounds · Developer: Peter Hussaini

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and the data associated with it in the Serenity Sounds app.

How to request deletion

Email peterhussaini.ph@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my Serenity Sounds data", from the email address you used to sign in.

We will verify the request and delete your data within 30 days, and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in record (email address; and, for Google sign-in, your name and profile information).
  • Any data linked to your account in our authentication provider (Firebase) and billing provider (RevenueCat).

What may be kept

  • Purchase / transaction records may be retained where required by law, tax rules, or Google Play's billing records (these are held by Google, not by us).
  • On-device data (your preferences and session history) lives only on your phone - it is removed when you sign out or uninstall the app, and is never sent to us.

Delete without contacting us

You can also remove all on-device data immediately by signing out in the app or uninstalling it. To revoke Google access, visit your Google Account permissions.

Questions? Email peterhussaini.ph@gmail.com.
